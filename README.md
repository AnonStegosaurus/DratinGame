### 1\. Features

  * **Arcade Survival:** Dodge aggressive and distracting chat messages while staying within the "safe zone" of your stream setup.
  * **Leveling System:** Gain experience (Views) and level up to choose powerful upgrades.
  * **Dynamic Difficulty:** The game difficulty scales over time to match your survival time.
  * **Hype Mechanic:** Manage your Hype bar. High Hype grants a shield; low Hype leads to game over.
  * **Fact-Checking Mini-Game:** Use your Critical Thinking to fact-check misleading messages.
  * **Chat Events:** React quickly to chat events by banning toxic users or granting VIP status to loyal viewers.

-----

### 2\. How to Play

The goal is to survive the longest while maintaining your **Hype** (HP).

1.  **Start the Stream:** Click **"Start Stream"** to begin.
2.  **Dodge Messages:** Move your streamer character (the avatar) to avoid falling chat messages.
      * **Good Messages (Green):** Increase your Hype.
      * **Bad Messages (Red/Toxic):** Decrease your Hype and apply a movement penalty.
3.  **Gain XP (Views):** Survive, dodge, and interact with the chat to gain experience and level up.
4.  **Upgrade:** When you level up, the game pauses, and the **Upgrade Screen** appears. Spend your earned levels to buy permanent power-ups.
5.  **Survive\!**

### 4\. Game Mechanics

#### Hype (Health)

The Hype bar represents your survival time.

  * If Hype reaches **100%**, you gain a **10-second invulnerability shield** (Hype Train\!).
  * If Hype drops to **0%**, the stream ends.

#### Views (XP)

Gained passively and through interaction. Collecting enough XP triggers a **Level Up**, allowing you to purchase upgrades.

-----

### 5\. Upgrades and Progression

Upgrades are purchased using your **Player Levels** as currency. The cost increases as you invest more into a specific upgrade, allowing for potentially infinite leveling.

| Upgrade Type | Effect | Cost Currency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stamina** | Increases movement speed and stamina capacity. | Player Levels |
| **Hype Train** | Increases XP multiplier and maximum Hype. | Player Levels |
| **Moderator** | Activates an auto-moderation feature to remove toxic messages automatically. | Player Levels |
| **Studies / Critical Thinking / Federal Police** | Upgrades related to the fact-checking mini-game. | Player Levels |
